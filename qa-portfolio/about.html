About Me

Quality is a discipline. I've practised it for years.

Get to know my background, my toolkit, and the modern skills I'm bringing into 2026.

I'm Suhasini, a Senior QA Automation Engineer who has spent the last six-plus years making sure enterprise software actually works — across web UIs, APIs, and the data warehouses behind them. I've built Selenium and TestNG frameworks from scratch, validated MicroStrategy reports against millions of rows of SQL, and wired Cucumber BDD suites into Jenkins so entire teams could trust their builds.

A couple of years ago I chose to take a planned career break. Rather than let my skills sit still, I treated the time as an investment: I've been deliberately modernizing my toolkit — moving from Selenium toward Playwright and Cypress, from Jenkins toward GitHub Actions, and adding TypeScript, contract testing, and accessibility testing to how I work.

Why the break makes me a better hire: I return with the same rigor I always had, plus a current, in-demand stack and the perspective to know which testing principles are timeless and which tools are simply the newest way to apply them.

I care about maintainable frameworks, meaningful assertions, and fast feedback loops. Whether it's a flaky legacy suite or a greenfield pipeline, I like leaving a codebase more testable than I found it.
